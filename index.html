Public Scope

Sanitized Outputs Only

The public frontend uses only approved public files. Internal model state, private configuration, database files, logs, backups, and execution logic are excluded.

Manifest-Driven

The public manifest describes the files available to the frontend, their purpose, type, size, and last update time.

No Execution

The public dashboard does not execute trades and does not provide capital deployment instructions.

Disclaimer

This dashboard is generated for research, monitoring, and decision-support purposes only. It is not financial advice, investment advice, or a recommendation to buy or sell any asset. Signals and rankings may be incomplete, delayed, or incorrect. Any live decision requires independent review, risk controls, and human approval.
