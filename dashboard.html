Adaptive Trend Intelligence Dashboard

This dashboard summarises public research-monitoring outputs from CTO Scanner. It is designed to show data freshness, public export status, candidate monitoring rows, and historical signal outcome records. It combines trend-flip signals, governance controls, risk posture, and candidate observations.

This dashboard is for informational monitoring only. It is not financial advice, not investment advice, not a recommendation, and not a trading signal. Assets are not ranked and not scored for public action. Data is refreshed by the scheduled scanner/export workflow when the nightly run completes successfully.

How to Read This Dashboard

  • Public status cards summarise operational readiness and export freshness — they are not market buy/sell signals.
  • Candidate rows are monitoring observations, not trade recommendations.
  • Outcome rows are historical tracking records, not proof of future performance.
  • Public CSV files are sanitised public exports and may not include internal review context.

Status labels such as red, blocked, or not_ready describe internal readiness, governance, or data-quality states. They should not be interpreted as market direction or trading recommendations. The public crypto coverage state remains no_public_exposure; internal research coverage is not shown as a public asset list. Freshness fields reflect the latest public export available to this static dashboard and are updated when the scheduled scanner/export workflow completes successfully.
